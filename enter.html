How Ghost Series works

  • Outer shell — heavyweight matte-black textile. Water & abrasion resistant.
  • Optical matrix core — side-emitting PMMA fiber-optic grid, LED-driven for uniform light.
  • Silhouette masking — a laser-etched layer reveals light only through programmed openings, forming the binary patterns.
  • Charging — USB-C universal, Qi wireless on bag & collar, app control for patterns. Limited & individually numbered.
